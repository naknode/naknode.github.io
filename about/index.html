About

Hey there, I’m Dan. I’m a Web Developer who mostly works within JavaScript and PHP. In the JavaScript side of things, I’m a big Node.js fan along with Vue.js and React.js. On the PHP side of thing, I’m a huge Laravel fan.

I have a penchant for test-driven development all thanks to the wonderful work of phpunit and Jest (and mocha/karma/chai).

If there’s one thing to know about me – I’m always learning. Whether it’s React Router to C# “First Steps” to Laravel Queues – there’s always something on my mind and I love it. I also love development conferences and empowering others through code.

I’m based in Birmingham, AL, so if you’re in the area – give me a shout on twitter or linkedin. Let’s talk recursion, unit testing, CI/CD and more!
